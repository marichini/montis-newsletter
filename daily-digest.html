The structural argument goes beyond T+0 or programmability: it challenges the entire economics of traditional post-trade back-office. Direct MDSA read-across: DLT's operational cost advantage becomes a powerful institutional sales vector. PostTrade360 June 5.

  • 🔥 Paxos becomes first SEC-registered blockchain clearing agency in the US (June 4) The SEC granted Paxos Securities Settlement Company (PSSC) registration as a clearing agency under Section 17A of the Exchange Act — making it the first and only blockchain-native firm approved as a registered clearing agency and CSD in the United States. 18-month temporary registration with exemptions; the result of 7 years of engagement with the SEC since a 2019 No-Action Letter. PSSC operated a settlement pilot with major financial institutions. This is a watershed for DLT-based post-trade infrastructure: a real blockchain-native CSD now operates legally inside the US regulatory perimeter, challenging the DTCC model. Federal Register / Paxos June 4.
